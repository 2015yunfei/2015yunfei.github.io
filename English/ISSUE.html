Issue的指令

ISSUE一共有6种指令：

  1. Write a response in which you discuss the extent to which you agree or disagree with the statement and explain your reasoning for the position you take. In developing and supporting your position, you should consider ways in which the statement might or might not hold true and explain how these considerations shape your position.

  2. Write a response in which you discuss the extent to which you agree or disagree with the recommendation and explain your reasoning for the position you take. In developing and supporting your position, describe specific circumstances in which adopting the recommendation would or would not be advantageous and explain how these examples shape your position.

  3. Write a response in which you discuss the extent to which you agree or disagree with the claim. In developing and supporting your position, be sure to address the most compelling reasons and/or examples that could be used to challenge your position.

  4. Write a response in which you discuss which view more closely aligns with your own position and explain your reasoning for the position you take. In developing and supporting your position, you should address both of the views presented.

  5. Write a response in which you discuss the extent to which you agree or disagree with the claim and the reason on which that claim is based.

  6. Write a response in which you discuss your views on the policy and explain your reasoning for the position you take. In developing and supporting your position, you should consider the possible consequences of implementing the policy and explain how these consequences shape your position.

 

判分流程

写作立场

我们可以写的立场理论上只有三个：

  1. 部分认同

  2. 中立（不推荐）

  3. 部分不认同

理论上，中立也是可以写的，因为只要我们可以在Issue中体现出来我们对事情的正反两面都有所思考就可以。但是考虑到中立这种思想本身行文比较困难，所以不推荐以中立的态度写作。

 

写作方法一

五段论：

  1. 开头

  2. 反

  3. 正

  4. 升

  5. 结尾

 

具体如何操作：

如果说我们的立场是有保留地同意A

  1. 第一段：有保留地同意A

  2. 第二段（反）：先不写自己的观点，先让步，对方说的什么什么东西是有道理的

  3. 第三段（正）：但是我方的观点是更有道理的

  4. 第四段（升）：站在一个更高的角度，我们分歧的本质是什么，站在这个更高的角度，我来告诉你为什么我比你更有道理

  5. 第五段：虽然我同意对方的观点有道理，但是我依然认为我的观点更有道理

 

如何写好升：

  1. 上位词

  2. 关键词

  3. 根本原因

 

范文：

Some people believe that college students should consider only their own talents and interests when choosing a field of study. Others believe that college students should base their choice of a field of study on the availability of jobs in that field.

Which field to choose to study might be the most important decision a young man needs to make during his or her teenage. While some people believe that college students should consider only their own talents and interests when choosing a field of study on the availability of jobs in that field. However, in my opinion, talent and interest are more important when deciding which field to study.

 

It is true that students who choose a major based on job availability could have better returns. Students pursing the hottest fields will find a good job and lead a life of above the middle class. Today, the hottest college major is computer science. Upon graduation, students in the field can find a well-paid position in a high-tech company easily. They can be financially independent and become a winner envied by other people. Seen in this light, it is a wise choice especially for students from disadvantaged backgrounds.

 

However, for most students, they should choose a field they have great interest in because it is the only way they can exploit their full potential and become the people they really want to be. The story of Steven Jobs is familiar to everyone. He took a calligraphy course when he was a freshman, which had nothing to do with finding a good job. But it was the calligraphy course that enlightened him to design better fonts for computers and ultimately impressed clients of Macintosh. If Jobs merely sought employment, he would never enter the class of calligraphy. The Apple we appreciate today would not exist at the very beginning.

 

The key to find a solution to the long-time dilemma between interest and money is how to understand the role of higher education in today’s world. What is the essence of higher education? What is the ultimate goal of a higher education institution? People have conflicting opinion on it. Though some people may argue that universities should make qualified workers for society, I agree with the old motto: higher education is to enrich one’s soul and make him or her find the true self. A student chases job opportunity is short-sighted because we all know work only takes 1/5 of out life time and what is popular today may be outdated when you graduate. Moreover, the development of online education and vocational education provides many opportunities for students to receive vocational training besides college study, but you will find no place than a college to let you understand what you love and who you want to be.

 

To sum up, although I admit job availability is an important indicator when choosing a field to study, interest and talent should play a more important role in students’ decision.

  2. Bad: 学生不应该以工作为导向选择专业，因为我身边有几个同学都选择了自己不喜欢的专业，他们很后悔。

  3. Good: 学生不应该以工作为导向选择专业，因为以工作为导向很容易让学生丧失学习热情，增加学习压力，导致他们表现不佳。在每一所大学都会见到这样的学生，因为现在CS最热门所以选择了这个专业，但他们不喜欢编程，认为编程很枯燥，所以学习非常痛苦，在职场上也没有竞争力，最后虚度大学四年时光。

 

TP: 政治家不应该盲目服从选民的意见。

  1. 政治家不应该盲目服从选民的意见。因为选民的看法十分容易受限于他自己的立场和他能获得的信息，他在做决策的时候几乎只考虑到自己或自己身边人的利益。与之对比，政治家可以咨询专业人士，可以获得内部信息，他们理应站在更高的角度为全社会做出合理的判断。例如，选民经常会认为花钱在科研或太空探索项目上是浪费，更希望钱花在教育、医疗这样的领域。但政治家应该意识到，投资科研和太空项目可以带来长期利益，可以增加国家的竞争力。因此，一味强调政治家服从选民意见可能损害全社会的利益。

第一篇文章基础款

In most profession and academic fields, imagination is more important than knowledge.

 

In many jobs and research fields, people wonder if imagination is more important than knowledge. Different people have different opinions about this issue. As far as I am concerned, knowledge is important, but imagination is even more crucial in what we do.

It is true that knowledge is very important in many fields. Knowledge gives people the basic information they need to do their work well. Think about a scientist: before they can do new research or question old ideas, they need to know what has already been found out. This knowledge helps them do their work carefully, learn from past mistakes, and use what other have already learned. Without this basic knowledge, even big projects can fail because of small mistakes or misunderstandings.

However, it is our creativity and fresh thinking, rather than just our knowledge, that leads to significant discoveries. Imagination allows us to come up with ideas that are truly new. In the world of art, many renowned artists did not rely only on their training but exploited their imagination instead. Artists like Van Gogh and Picasso introduced new approaches and styles. Their art was not just a mirror to the world; it offered a unique perspective. Similarly, in the business world, imagination enables innovative thinking and technological revolution. Take Steve Jobs and his iPhone as an example. He did not just base it on existing technology, but explored what could be possible in the future. He imagined a phone that combined communication, entertainment, and work. This version set Apple apart and led to groundbreaking products.

The core of this issue is how we understand imagination. Some people think imagination is just a sudden idea and assert that knowledge is more important because it is the base of everything. But I believe imagination helps any field, whether it is scientific research or business project, move forward. Imagination does more than just bring new ideas; it links different kinds of knowledge and lets them work together. Without imagination, knowledge cannot change or grow; with imagination, knowledge can keep improving and combine with other ideas to make something new. We should never underestimate how important imagination is.

In conclusion, even though knowledge is very important in work and study, I think imagination is even more important. With imagination, scientists make big discoveries, businesses try new things, and society keeps moving forward.

第二篇文章基础款

In order for any work of art-for example, a film, a novel, a poem, or a song-to have merit, it must be understandable to most people.

 

In the world of books, movies, and music, there is a debate about whether art is valuable if most people do not understand it. People from different backgrounds have different views on this. While some great works can be hard to understand, I still think the vales of an artwork is related to its understandability.

 

Certainly, some artworks might be hard for the general public to understand, but they are sill important. These works might break traditional rules, introduce new ideas, or be deeply connected to a specific culture or time, making them harder for everyone to appreciate. For example, James Joyce’s “Ulysses” can be confusing because of its unique style and references. Yet, its complexity and depth have made it one of the most respected books, showing that being easy to understand is not the only measure of value.

 

However, if we look at the history of art, there is a clear link between how easy a work is to understand and its importance. In movies, films like Steven Spielberg’s “E.T. The Extra-Terrestrial” or Frank Capra’s “It’s a Wonderful Life” are loved not just for their quality but because they beautiful show themes like love and hope that many people can relate to. Similarly, paintings like Van Gogh’s “starry Night” impress people with their unique colors and emotions and provoke them to think, even if they are not art experts.

 

The key issue here is how we understand the “merit” of art. Some say that the more complex and deep a piece is, the better, implying that art is mainly for elites. However, I think the real beauty of art is its ability to connect with many people. If most people cannot relate to a piece of artwork, it loses its power to move people broadly. On the other hand, art that speaks to many can bridge gaps and bring people together. Therefore, art that is hard for most to understand might be missing something important.

 

To sum up, while the value of art is not just about how clear it is, being understandable plays a big role in its lasting impact. Art reflects out world and emotions. Its true value shines when it reaches and moves many, not just a few.

第一篇

All college and university students would benefit from spending at least one semester studying in a foreign country.

Write a response in which you discuss the extent to which you agree or disagree with the statement and explain your reasoning for the position you take. In developing and supporting your position, you should consider ways in which the statement might or might not hold true and explain how these considerations shape your position.

 

原文

Whether students in colleges and universities would benefit from at least one semester studying in a foreign country has been a hot topic today. While some argue that in the present and future, everybody has more chance to have a life experience in other country than ever before, which absolutely would bring a lot of benefits to students. However, I hold the opposite view that students should devote themselves in their own major, believing that studying in other country is a kind of wasting time and money.

It is true that since the rapid development of economy and transportation, almost every person get the chance to have an experience in other countries. Someone believes that students in colleges and universities, if they spend at least one semester in a foreign country, will develop their ability to live independently in a foreign country while understanding how society works in other countries which will helps them get a better understanding of the world and have a clearer picture of their own life.

As far as I concerned, students who want to have abroad learning life is about to face thousands of problems, Such as the limited time and the lack of financial support. On the one hand, in today's era of industrialization, every subject, even the humanities, has a huge knowledge hierarchy which consists massive knowledge that students can hardly acquire most of them without doubt. In this circumstance, it is widely regarded unwise to spend one semester, sometimes even more, in an unfamiliar country because as students, they should take the completion of the study of professional knowledge as the most important task. On the other hand, the source of money supporting this journey is also a big trouble. Students from poor family have to support themselves, both tuition and living expenses, surely having less chance to learning outside compared with the rich, which is going to widen the gap between the rich and the poor.

The core of this issue is how we understand the true meaning of benefit. Some people think the life in a new country getting connection with different people in different language is helpful to students’ growth process.
